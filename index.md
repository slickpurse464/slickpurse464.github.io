## 💡 About Noshy

AI agents often forget facts from past chats. Noshy solves this. It acts as a long-term memory bank for your digital assistant. The software saves important details from your conversations and finds them later when you need them.

This application uses smart search tools to pull up relevant data instantly. It runs directly on your computer without complicated setup steps. You keep full control over your files and private data.

## 📋 Requirements

Before you start, check that your computer meets these standards:

*   **Operating System**: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   **Memory**: At least 4GB of RAM.
*   **Storage**: 500MB of free disk space.
*   **Internet Access**: Required for initial setup and updates.

## 🛠️ Configuring Your Memory

When you run the app, the main window will appear. You need to connect your AI agent to the memory storage.

1. Click the "Settings" icon in the top right corner.
2. Select the "Storage" tab.
3. Choose a folder on your drive where Noshy will save memory files.
4. Click "Save Changes."

Now the agent can read and write to your chosen folder. Any information you store remains accessible only to your agents.

## 🔍 How It Works

Noshy performs two main tasks to help your agent.

### Data Extraction
When your agent encounters new facts, Noshy pulls that information out. It identifies names, dates, preferences, and tasks. It saves these as plain text notes.

### Hybrid Search
Standard search tools use keywords. Noshy uses hybrid search. This means it looks for keywords but also understands the meaning behind your request. If you ask about "last week's meeting," the system finds the files even if the word "meeting" does not appear in the title.

## 🛡️ Privacy

Your data stays on your machine. Noshy does not send your personal logs to a central server. You keep your files safe in the local folder you selected during the setup process. You can delete or edit these files at any time using your standard file manager.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Does this require coding skills?
No. You do not need to write code. The installer manages the setup process for you.

Yes. Go to the folder you chose in the settings menu. You can delete the files inside to wipe the memory.

### How do I update the application?
When a update exists, the app displays a notification. Click the link in that notification to download the new version. The installer will replace the old files with the new ones automatically.

### Is my subscription data stored?
Noshy focuses on memory storage for agents. It does not track your subscription status or billing information.
